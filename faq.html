Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why should we work with GECTO?
GECTO offers complete digital transformation – from websites and apps to e‑commerce and gamified marketing. Every client gets a custom BrandQuest game to boost engagement. We also provide lifetime maintenance and security patching.
2. Why choose GECTO over others?
We are rooted in African strength and legacy. Our unique edge: mandatory BrandQuest games, SkillForge edutainment, proactive patching, and a commitment to moving businesses from paper to digital databases.
3. What is the future of GECTO?
By 2032, GECTO will be one of the Africa's leading software powerhouse, with HQ in Gwanda. We will digitise at least 100 traditional businesses and bring SkillForge games to schools and most rural areas.
4. Who is GECTO's target audience?
Everyone! Traditional businesses on paper records, kids and youths (5–25) for SkillForge games, and startups/SMEs that need websites, apps, and gamified marketing. We serve all ages and sectors.
5. Why do people need GECTO?
Because the world is digital. Paper‑based businesses are invisible. Young people need practical skills. GECTO bridges the gap with affordable, scalable solutions, gamified education, and e‑commerce.
