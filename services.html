🌍 The "ONE DIGITALISED WORLD" Project

GECTO’s flagship initiative, led by founder Stenford Mpofu (Mr Ceo), aims to completely digitise Zimbabwe’s economy by 2032. This 7‑year roadmap (2026–2032) focuses on moving traditional businesses from paper books to intelligent databases, e‑commerce, and cloud systems. We target underserved communities, providing affordable software, training, and ongoing support. The project includes building a network of digital hubs in rural areas, offering free basic websites to 100 small businesses, and integrating gamified learning into schools. By 2031, we will establish our headquarters in Gwanda, turning it into a Southern African tech innovation hub. Our goal is to create a blueprint for digital transformation that can be replicated across Africa. Join us in building a future where every person and business is connected, empowered, and ready for the global digital economy.
