Our Game Strategy: Learn & Earn

SkillForge – Real Skills, Real Fun

We build educational games that teach financial literacy, coding basics, supply chain management, and entrepreneurship. Children and youth learn by playing – gaining practical skills for the future economy.

BrandQuest – Marketing Through Play

Every business that partners with GECTO receives a custom promotional game. Customers earn loyalty points, discounts, and rewards while engaging with your brand. This turns passive users into active fans.

Meet Mr Ceo – Stenford Mpofu

Born in Gwanda and raised in Beitbridge, Stenford completed his studies in Bulawayo. In 2022, he founded GEC Industries, and in early 2026 he launched GECTO with a single mission: to bring technology to underserved communities. His bold vision, "The One Digitalised World", aims to revolutionise Zimbabwe's digital landscape by 2032. Through GECTO, he is building a legacy of innovation, inclusion, and African excellence.
